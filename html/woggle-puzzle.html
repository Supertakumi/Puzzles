Instructions

  1. Click on the letters embedded in the boxes to create words.
  2. Click on 'Submit Word' button to submit your word.
  3. If you submit a correct word, you would get 2 points for each consonant and 1 point for each vowel.
  4. Submission of an incorrect word would not be accepted and would not increment your score.
  5. You must use a word only once. Duplicates are not allowed.
  6. You will have 90 seconds after the game starts. The game will start once you click the 'Start' button below.
